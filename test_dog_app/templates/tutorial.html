Tutorial


First, you must create an account before viewing your pets and their entries. Simply click the register button which will bring you to the register form.


Fill in the blanks with your information.
NOTE: the email field could be left blank if you choose to leave out that information. You can edit your profile later so there is no need to rush.


1) You could use this navigation bar to go back and fourth throughout this website
2) Click on your username to access your user profile. There, you could edit your information such as username, profile picture, and email.
3) You can begin by adding your Pet's name using the button.


Type in your pet's name and add your pet to my database :)


You will see your pet's name display on the page.
NOTE: This page is unique to you. No one else will be able to view your pets UNLESS you decide to share your post.


Press the add entry button to write about your pet


1) Fill out the biography form. Spacing does matter, however, pressing tab will NOT indent your work so please consider pressing space 4 times to indent. I currently do not have a way around this.
2) You could choose to upload a photo of your pet. But, you are limited to 1 photo per pet. You may edit your photo afterwards so do not be afraid to choose.
3) Enter your masterpiece by pressing the submit button
NOTE: Similar to your pets, NO ONE will be able to see your pet's entries unless you decide to share them.


Once you submitted your entry, you can take a look at the share and edit entry option. Click on the edit entry to change your submission or photo and if you would like to share your posts please click that share button.
You can check your community submissions by clicking "Your Community Profile Tab" where you will be able to view, edit and remove your community entries.
